# Founder Decision System — PRD

## Problem
Founders are overwhelmed by competing ideas, unclear direction, and no structured process to validate and execute. The manual alternative — repeated questioning, contradiction-spotting, option comparison, risk assessment — is slow and generic.

## Target User
Founders, entrepreneurs, business owners, and coaches who need to move from confusion to a concrete, personalised execution plan for one business idea.

## Core Objects
- **Decision Credit** — a purchased unit allowing one full decision journey
- **Decision Session** — one workspace per idea, spanning all three engines
- **Engine Response** — every answer a founder gives inside a session
- **AI Output** — generated reports (Core Goal, Goal Pathway, Blueprint30, Final Report)
- **Decision Vault** — the founder's personal library of completed sessions and reports

## MVP Must-Haves (v1)
- [ ] Credit packages selectable at checkout (3 tiers by budget)
- [ ] Purchase flow creates account + allocates credits
- [ ] New session consumes one credit and opens Engine 1
- [ ] Engine 1 (Clarity & Direction): structured question flow → AI generates Core Goal report
- [ ] Engine 2 (Pathway & Validation): question flow → AI generates Goal Pathway report
- [ ] Engine 3 (Execution & Results): question flow → AI generates Blueprint30 + Final Report
- [ ] Progress auto-saved; session resumable at last completed step
- [ ] Decision Vault lists all sessions; each report viewable and downloadable (PDF)
- [ ] Demo mode: seed data visible without login

## Non-Goals (v1)
- Long-term coaching, habit tracking, or business performance monitoring
- Team/multi-user workspaces
- Subscription billing
- In-app messaging or community

## Success Criteria
A real paying customer creates an account, purchases a credit package, completes all three engines for one business idea, and receives — without any manual help — a downloadable Final Founder Decision Report stored in their Decision Vault.
